Experience Tranquility

Using SceneManager, I created 3 different environments. The welcome screen is playful, setting off firecrackers wherever you click. The rainy scene is relaxing, playing ambient sound of a rainstorm, and allowing you to control the thunder with a button. The creepy scene is a 40 mintue walk through the forest I recorded, with creepy and disturbing music available for optional play.
