A Great Ball of Nothing

This project was based on the quote by the Persian poet Saadi Shirazi. Each "string" produced by a mouse click lives a finite life. The remnants or memory of that string are captured on the canvas. Some strings overlap, much like the lives of people. However, eventually they must all come to an end.
