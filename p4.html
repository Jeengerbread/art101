Quantum Flucuations

I found it intriguing that computers couldn't generate truly random numbers. Using data from Australian National University's Quantum Random Number Generator, this project visualizes random flucuations in a quantum "canvas". The flucuations, measured in a vacuum chamber in a lab at the Australian National University, are used to determine the positions of each point. The canvas is a rudimentary way of listening to the universe speak.
